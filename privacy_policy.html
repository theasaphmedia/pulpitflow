Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 18, 2026

1. Introduction

PulpitFlow is a sermon preparation and preaching tool developed by TAI, founded by Solomon Stephen. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using PulpitFlow, you agree to the terms described here.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address. Your password is never stored by us — it is managed securely by Supabase Authentication using industry-standard encryption.

b) Profile Information

You may optionally provide your name, ministry title, church name, denomination, city, and country. This information is used solely to personalise your in-app experience and is never sold or shared with third parties.

c) Sermon & Ministry Content

All sermon content, notes, tags, series, and related data you create belongs entirely to you. This content is never used to train AI models or shared with any third party.

d) Bible Highlights & Reading Data

Scripture passages you highlight or bookmark are stored privately in your account and are never shared.

e) Word Study Searches

When you use the Word Study feature, only the word or phrase you search is sent to our secure backend — which then queries the Anthropic Claude AI. Your sermons, profile, and personal data are never included in these requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the PulpitFlow application
  • Sync your sermons and data securely across sessions
  • Power AI-assisted features (Word Study) through our secure backend
  • Personalise your experience within the app
  • Respond to support requests sent to pulpitflow@gmail.com
  • Improve app performance and fix issues

4. Third-Party Services

PulpitFlow uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

Supabase

Provides secure data storage and user authentication. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Supabase is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified. See: supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic Claude (Word Study feature)

Powers the Word Study biblical research feature. Only the word or phrase you search is sent via our secure server — no personal data, sermon content, or account information is transmitted. See: anthropic.com/privacy

API.Bible

Provides Scripture text retrieval. Only the requested Bible reference is sent. No personal data is transmitted. See: scripture.api.bible/privacy

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • All data is encrypted at rest within Supabase's secure infrastructure
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) ensures each user can only ever access their own data
  • The Anthropic API key is stored as a server-side secret — never exposed to the app or its users
  • Code obfuscation is applied to the app to resist reverse engineering
Your sermons are yours. No one at TAI, and no AI system, can read your sermon content. It is protected by encryption and strict access controls.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, all associated data — including your profile, sermons, and highlights — will be permanently deleted within 30 days. To request deletion, contact us at pulpitflow@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: View your data at any time within the app
  • Correction: Update your profile information in-app at any time
  • Export: Use the "Export Backup" feature in Settings to download all your sermon data
  • Deletion: Request full account and data deletion by emailing pulpitflow@gmail.com
  • Portability: Your exported backup is in a standard format you can keep indefinitely

8. Children's Privacy

PulpitFlow is designed for pastors, ministers, and ministry workers. It is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
